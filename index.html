Our Mission

Promoting Quality & Excellence in Dermatologic Care

The Connecticut Society of Dermatology and Dermatologic Surgery is the professional home for Connecticut's dermatologists — connecting physicians to each other, to national organizations, and to the legislative processes that shape their practice.

Founded in 1968, the Society advocates for patients and physicians at the state and federal level, facilitates continuing medical education, and supports members through every stage of their career.

About the Society
Our Values
  • Patients FirstCompassion and excellence in dermatologic care are at the heart of everything we do.
  • ProfessionalismAdhering to the highest clinical and ethical standards set forth by the AMA and AAD.
  • CollaborationWorking with specialty organizations nationally with collegiality and transparency.
  • Lifelong LearningPursuing knowledge and continuous improvement at every stage of a physician's career.
  • Social ResponsibilityCommunity leadership and stewardship as hallmarks of dermatology in Connecticut.
