Mission

Promoting Quality & Excellence in Dermatologic Care

The Connecticut Society of Dermatology and Dermatologic Surgery was established to advance the practice of dermatology in Connecticut through education, research, and advocacy at the state and federal level.

The Society connects its members to national organizations, legislative processes, continuing medical education, and the broader professional community of dermatologists practicing across the state.

About the Society
Values
  • Patients FirstCompassion, caring and listening are at the heart of delivering the highest-quality dermatologic care in Connecticut.
  • ProfessionalismAdhering to an uncompromising code of clinical and ethical standards set forth by the AMA and American Academy of Dermatology.
  • CollaborationWorking together with other medical specialty organizations with mutual respect, collegiality and transparency.
  • Lifelong LearningPursuing knowledge and continuous improvement through self-assessment and ongoing evaluation.
  • Social ResponsibilityCommunity leadership, volunteerism and stewardship in service of public health in Connecticut and beyond.

Membership

Membership is open to board-certified and board-eligible dermatologists practicing in Connecticut. Dues are tiered by years in practice.

Regular Membership $450 / year
First Year in PracticeNew practitioners $175 / year
Second Year in Practice $260 / year
Third Year in Practice $350 / year
Senior MemberAge 67 or older, fewer than 20 hours per week $175 / year
