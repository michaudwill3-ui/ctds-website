About the Society

Who We Are

The Connecticut Society of Dermatology and Dermatologic Surgery — serving Connecticut's dermatologists since 1968.

Our Story

A Society Built by Physicians, for Physicians

The Connecticut Society of Dermatology and Dermatologic Surgery was founded to unite the state's dermatologists under a common mission: to promote excellence in patient care, advance the science of dermatology, and advocate for physicians and their patients at both the state and federal level.

For decades, the Society has served as the professional home for Connecticut's dermatologists — connecting members to national organizations including the American Academy of Dermatology, the American Society for Dermatologic Surgery, the American Board of Dermatology, and the American Osteopathic College of Dermatology.

The Society is led by an elected Board of Officers and a volunteer Executive Committee of Connecticut dermatologists who give their time to advance the profession, engage the legislature, and support the next generation of physicians entering practice in our state.
