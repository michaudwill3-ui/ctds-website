About the Society

Connecticut Society of Dermatology

The Connecticut Society of Dermatology and Dermatologic Surgery — serving the state's dermatologists since 1968.

The Society

A Professional Society for Connecticut's Dermatologists

The Connecticut Society of Dermatology and Dermatologic Surgery was established to unite the state's dermatologists under a shared commitment to excellence in patient care, the advancement of dermatologic science, and the representation of physicians at the state and federal legislative level.

The Society is affiliated with national organizations including the American Academy of Dermatology, the American Society for Dermatologic Surgery, the American Board of Dermatology, and the American Osteopathic College of Dermatology, ensuring that Connecticut members have access to the full breadth of national resources, advocacy, and education.

Leadership is provided by an elected Board of Officers and a volunteer Executive Committee of Connecticut dermatologists who represent the Society in legislative, educational, and professional matters across the state and nation.
